# Ballston Spa Zoning Logic Framework
Version 1.0

## Purpose
Evaluate whether proposed development aligns with zoning intent and protects village character.

## Core Zoning Principles

### Scale Compatibility
Buildings should match surrounding neighborhood scale.

High risk indicators:
- Height significantly exceeds nearby buildings
- Excessive mass or footprint

### Density Compatibility
Density should reflect neighborhood pattern.

High risk indicators:
- Large apartment complexes in single-family areas

### Use Compatibility
Uses should align with zoning district intent.

High risk indicators:
- Commercial use in residential area without transition

### Design Compatibility
Architecture should reinforce village character.

High risk indicators:
- Large blank walls
- Excessive parking frontage

## Output Categories
Aligned  
Moderate Concern  
High Concern  
Severe Conflict
