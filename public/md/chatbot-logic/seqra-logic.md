# SEQRA Environmental Review Logic Framework
Version 1.0

## Purpose
Evaluate environmental impact under New York SEQRA principles.

## Environmental Impact Categories

### Traffic Impact
Evaluate:
- Increase in vehicle trips
- Safety impact

High risk indicators:
- Significant traffic increase
- Safety degradation

### Stormwater Impact
Evaluate:
- Runoff increase
- Flood risk

High risk indicators:
- Large impervious surfaces

### Community Character Impact
Evaluate:
- Visual impact
- Neighborhood compatibility

### Infrastructure Impact
Evaluate:
- Sewer capacity
- Utility load

## SEQRA Risk Levels
Low Impact  
Moderate Impact  
Potentially Significant Impact  
Significant Adverse Impact
