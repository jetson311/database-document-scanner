# Ballston Spa Comprehensive Plan Logic Framework
Version 1.0

## Core Mission
Evaluate whether decisions align with Ballston Spa's comprehensive plan priorities:
- Preserve historic character
- Protect village scale and identity
- Maintain walkability and quality of life
- Support downtown vitality
- Encourage compatible growth

## Scoring System
0–2 = Supports plan  
3–5 = Neutral  
6–8 = Conflicts with plan  
9–10 = Strong conflict  

Total score interpretation:
0–15 = Strong alignment  
16–30 = Moderate concern  
31–50 = High concern  
51–70 = Severe conflict  

## Categories

### Historic Preservation
Evaluate:
- Historic building preservation
- Architectural compatibility
- Height relative to surroundings

High risk indicators:
- Demolition of historic buildings
- Incompatible architectural design
- Excessive height

### Community Character
Evaluate:
- Neighborhood compatibility
- Density consistency
- Visual dominance

High risk indicators:
- Large-scale buildings in low-scale areas
- Sudden density increase

### Village Identity
Evaluate:
- Architectural fit
- Village-scale appropriateness

High risk indicators:
- Strip mall design
- Generic suburban architecture

### Walkability
Evaluate:
- Pedestrian safety
- Sidewalk connectivity

High risk indicators:
- Car-dominated design
- Unsafe pedestrian conditions

### Downtown Vitality
Evaluate:
- Economic support of downtown
- Pedestrian activity impact

### Infrastructure and Environmental Compatibility
Evaluate:
- Stormwater impact
- Infrastructure capacity

### Comprehensive Plan Alignment
Evaluate:
- Alignment with village vision
